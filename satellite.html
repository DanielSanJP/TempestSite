    Satellite Features

    This satellite features a Propulsion system, a unit that utilizes interactions between orthogonal electric fields to generate a unidirectional thrust. This is a propellant-less system that will perform as long as it has a source of electricity which not only significantly reduces the operational costs related to fuel consumption but is also more environmentally friendly, which lines up with our global sustainability ideals.

    This propellant-less system ensures that the satellite can maintain continuous propulsion and manoeuvrings as long as it has a source of electricity which allow for extended mission durations and flexibility in mission plannings. The Advanced propulsion system is a strategic advantage that provides a suite of benefits tailored to meet the unique needs of our target market. Its innovative design not only separates our satellite from the traditional ways but also positions it at the forefront of space exploration technology.

    This system embodies the future of deep-space exploration, providing unparalleled mission longevity, flexibility, and reliability. Its adoption is not merely a choice of technology but a forward-looking investment in the success of long-term, complex, and ambitious space endeavours.
